Frequently Asked Questions

How does StudyGlade homework help work?

Students post their questions with a budget. Expert tutors bid, and once assigned, they provide step‑by‑step answers. You pay only when satisfied.

What subjects are available?

We cover over 100 subjects: math, calculus, statistics, nursing, engineering, computer science, business, economics, biology, chemistry, physics, history, literature, and more.

Are tutors available 24/7?

Yes, our tutors are located across time zones and are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to help with urgent assignments.

How do you verify tutor quality?

All tutors undergo a rigorous application process, including subject exams, portfolio review, and identity verification. Only 15% of applicants are approved.

Absolutely. Payments are held in escrow and released only after you confirm the answer meets your requirements. We use Paystack for secure transactions.
